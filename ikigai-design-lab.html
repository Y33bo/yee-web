About

Ikigai Design Lab was founded in June 2023 as a creative outlet for work that is different to my usual design and illustration style. It is also a space for experimental work with things such as generative AI, building apps, and other miscellaneous creative endeavours. Occasionally I will produce goods for this over on Etsy

Currently working on the design and build of an app to help encourage people to form healthier eating habits and make little changes to help improve their overall health. As part of this project I have been learning more about backend development, and conducting user research. My plan is to make it an open source project in the near future once the first version is ready to go live.
