What is Couchbase Capella?

Capella is Couchbase's cloud SaaS offering. It's the easiest and fastest way to begin with Couchbase and to eliminate ongoing database management efforts. You can find out more here or try it out here . Customers include Paypal, PepsiCo, Sky, Tesco, UPS, Amadeus, Cisco and GE.
